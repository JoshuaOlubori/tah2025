 {This report addresses profitability concerns facing AdventureWorks by providing a  data-driven diagnosis of its sales performance and product portfolio. Utilizing transaction-level data, the analysis employs channel comparison, customer segmentation (RFM), and product-level margin analysis to identify the core drivers of depressed net income.  Key findings reveal a profit paradox: the high-revenue Reseller channel operates at significantly lower, often negative, net margins, as opposed to the consistently high profitability of the Online channel. Customer segmentation confirms that value is highly concentrated within a small subset of loyal patrons, while product analysis identifies a clear division between high-margin "hero" products and consistent loss-makers driven by aggressive discounting behavior. The resulting strategic recommendations focus on immediate profitability audits for low-margin SKUs, implementing strict discount controls, and redesigning reseller incentives based on net margin rather than gross revenue. This research provides a analytical framework that guides the transition from a revenue-driven to a sustainably profitable operational strategy.}
